Stick Calibration

To calibrate your sticks, ensure both sticks are fully centered, then press start. Slowly rotate both sticks fully 4-5 times. The 'Save' button will become enabled when enough data has been captured to facilitate proper calibration. Press 'Stop' to cancel calibration.
