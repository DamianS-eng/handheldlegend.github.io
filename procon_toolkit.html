Pro Controller
Configurator

To configure your device, first enable pairing mode by holding the small button on top of the controller.

Pair the controller with your device in your bluetooth settings.

Next, click the 'connect' button to select your device.

If you get a permission dialogue, grant permission, then click 'connect' again.
